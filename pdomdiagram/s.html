    Description

    TEV protease is a highly sequence-specific cysteine protease from Tobacco Etch Virus. It is a member of the PA clan of chymotrypsin-like proteases. Due to its high sequence specificity, TEV protease is frequently used for the controlled cleavage of fusion proteins in vitro and in vivo. Wikipedia
